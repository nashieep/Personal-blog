What is Afro beats?

Afrobeat was developed in Nigeria in the late 1960s by Fela Kuti

ˈa-frō-ˌbēt

Afrobeat is a Nigerian music genre that involves the combination of West African musical styles and American funk, jazz, and soul influences, with a focus on chanted vocals, complex intersecting rhythms, and percussion.


Why Afro beats?

Afrobeat, as a musical genre, pioneered the inclusivity of the Africans in the diaspora

Afrobeats is jubilant party music that draws on West African sources — hybrid languages, propulsive rhythms.
